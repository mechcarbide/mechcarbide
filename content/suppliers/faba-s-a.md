## 📌 Why Choose FABA S.A.?

**Best For**: Industrial furniture manufacturers, sawmills, and large-scale woodworking facilities seeking advanced coated cutting tools and comprehensive factory-level regrinding services.

* **Anti-Burn Wiper Knives for High-Volume Multi-Rip Saws**: FABA specifically engineers their multi-rip saw blades with **integrated wiper knives (rakers)** built directly into the highest-quality steel body. These wipers actively eject sawdust and chips from the kerf as the blade spins. For the buyer, this ensures the blade runs significantly **cooler**, eliminating material burning and chip blocking, while delivering a smooth surface that often bypasses the need for secondary planing.
* **V-Marked Laser Cuts for Acoustic Control**: Industrial woodworking environments are notoriously loud. Therefore, FABA developed **saw blades marked with "V."** These blades feature strategically placed, proprietary **laser cuts** that alter the vibration frequency of the steel plate, reducing operational noise by up to **50%**. This creates a much safer and more comfortable shop environment without compromising cutting stability.
* **Factory-Level Regrinding & Regeneration Service**: Worn-out diamond or carbide tools represent a huge sunken cost if not maintained properly. FABA doesn't just sell tools; they operate a professional authorized service center equipped to **regrind HSS, HM, and DP tips**. Utilizing **electro-erosion (EDG)** and **CAD-CAM** systems, they can restore tools to their exact original profiles. Furthermore, they offer "**regeneration**" services, replacing missing or damaged carbide/diamond tips entirely, and return the tools dynamically balanced to **G 2.5** class with a measurement certificate.
* **Integrated Automated Production (AIMS) & Quality Control**: In mass production, varying tool quality between batches can lead to unpredictable finish qualities and machine jams. Operating from a massive **40,000 m²** facility, FABA has heavily invested in laser and electronic control systems, including their **Automatic Shank Tools Production System (AIMS)**. This high level of automation, paired with optical computer-operated measurement machines, ensures that every single batch of router bits or saw blades maintains identical performance characteristics and precise cutting geometries.
