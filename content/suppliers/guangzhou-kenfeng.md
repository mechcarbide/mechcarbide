## 📌 Why Choose Tiger (Kenfeng)?

**Best For**: Professional furniture manufacturers and woodworking shops looking for high-end "no-burr" finishes on sensitive laminates without the premium price tag of European brands.

* **Tailored Carbide Grades for Specific Materials**: Instead of using a "one-size-fits-all" carbide for every tool, **Tiger selects specific carbide grades based on the type of board being processed** (e.g., solid wood vs. abrasive man-made boards). This material matching ensures stable hardness and prevents the premature chipping often seen in generic tools.
* **Robotic Precision & Sharpness**: Tiger utilizes **fully enclosed robotic production lines**. This automation ensures that the grinding angles of every cutter are consistent and razor-sharp, which is critical for maintaining high-speed cutting stability and precision over long production runs.
* **No Edge-Chipping on Sensitive Boards**: A major pain point for woodworkers is "edge-bursting" or burrs on **melamine and other laminates**. Tiger bits are designed with optimized chip evacuation, ensuring that workpieces come out with mirror-smooth surfaces and clean edges, eliminating the need for secondary sanding or rework.
* **Proven Durability**: By using high-rigidity steel for the tool bodies and high-impact resistance carbide tips, Tiger tools are engineered to be tougher than standard cutters, significantly extending the replacement cycle and lowering the overall cost per cut.
