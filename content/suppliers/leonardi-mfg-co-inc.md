Leonardi Manufacturing is a family-operated company in Weedsport, NY, known for producing **replacement parts for stump grinders and tree care equipment.** Its product range includes stump grinder teeth, cutting wheels, wood chipper blades, and tree rigging components, supported by decades of industry experience. While the company continues to manufacture its legacy handbag frames, its core strength today lies in delivering reliable solutions for arborists and tree care professionals. 

Backed by in-house expertise and a broad supplier network, Leonardi also provides metalworking capabilities such as stamping, CNC machining, and robotic welding to support diverse customer needs.

## Product Categories

### 1. Stump Grinder Teeth & Wheels

![Leonardi stump grinder teeth and wheels](/uploads/leonardi-product-range.jpg "Leonardi Stump Grinder Teeth & Wheels")

* Leonardi Manufacturing produces a full range of stump grinder teeth designed as durable replacements for **Vermeer, Bobcat, Bandit, Carlton, Rayco, Dosko, Toro, Morbark, and other OEM machines**. These American-made teeth, along with matching pockets and bolts, are built to mount directly onto existing grinder wheels. 
* The company also offers several cutting wheel systems, including the **M1 Silver Wheel, M1 Blueshark™ Cutting System, and Phantom Wheel™**.

### 2. Tree Rigging Equipment

Leonardi manufactures high-performance tree rigging equipment for climbers, riggers, and tree care professionals who need reliable tools for pulling, lowering, and controlled removals. All products are made in the USA, with the Hitch-Wrap system providing a versatile and cost-effective solution for common rigging needs.

### 3. Wood Chipper Blades

The company supplies chipper blades compatible with a wide range of tree and brush chipper models. All blades are produced in the USA using grade A8 chipper steel sourced domestically. Corresponding anvils are also available in multiple shapes and sizes to match specific machine requirements.

### 4. Tree Care Tools

Leonardi offers additional tools and accessories designed to support general tree care operations, complementing its stump grinding, rigging, and chipper blade product lines.
