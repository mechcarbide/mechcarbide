Founded in 1998 by Laurent Denis and Monique Villancourt, Denis Cimaf is a global leader in high-quality **mulching attachments** for vegetation management and land clearing. The company designs and manufactures innovative mulchers for skid steers and excavators, including the renowned **DAF, DXF, and DAH mulching heads.** 

Denis Cimaf became **part of Morbark, LLC in 2019**, strengthening Morbark's position in the vegetation management and land-clearing equipment market. With over 500 employees, Denis Cimaf products are produced at the Morbark, LLC facility in Wooster. As part of the **Alamo Group**'s Vegetation Management Division, Denis Cimaf benefits from the group's global expertise and commitment to sustainable land management solutions alongside sister brands like **Morbark and Rayco**.

## Product Categories

### 1. Mulching Heads

* **DAF Front-Mounted Mulchers:** Industrial-grade brushcutting and mulching systems for compact loaders and dedicated forestry carriers. Ideal for high-efficiency vegetation control.
* **DAH Boom-Mounted Mulchers:** Powerful boom-mounted systems for excavators and carriers with articulated masts, built for demanding forestry and land management tasks.
* **DXF Front-Mounted Mulchers:** Robust, high-power brushcutting and mulching heads designed for excavators and articulated-mast carriers, delivering consistent and reliable performance.
