## Quick Facts

**Company Name**: Shareate Tools Ltd. (苏州新锐合金工具股份有限公司)

**Location**: Headquarters in Suzhou Industrial Park; Production bases in Suzhou, Wuhan, Zhuzhou, and Guiyang.

**Established**: 2005

**Business Type**: International Manufacturing Service Provider (Publicly listed on SSE: 688257)

**Global Presence**: Over 40 subsidiaries/branches worldwide, including the US, Australia, Canada, Chile, and South Korea.

**MOQ Policy**: Supports large-scale industrial supply and offers a "flexible production system" for customized design and manufacturing.

**Export Experience**: Yes. Direct supplier to global mining giants like BHP, Rio Tinto, and FMG.

**Key Advantage**: A "one-stop" industrial chain that produces both the tungsten carbide material and the finished rock drilling tools.

## 📦 Products & Customization Capabilities

**Rock Drilling Tools**: Mining tri-cone bits (6 3/4" to 13 3/4"), DTH (Down-the-Hole) bits, water well bits, single roller cone bits, reamers, and raise bore tools.

**Tungsten Carbide Products**: Carbide buttons, PDC substrates, carbide rods, plates, and shield machine tools.

**Precision Tooling**: Cold heading dies and powder metallurgy molds.

**Customization (OEM/ODM)**: Highly responsive to specific geological conditions. Their CNC machining centers allow for rapid customized design of drill bits and series to meet unique project requirements.

## 📌 Why Choose Shareate Tools?

**Best For**: Large-scale mining operators, international drilling contractors, and distributors seeking "Tier-1" quality rock tools with local technical support.

* **Integrated Upstream & Downstream Chain**: Unlike many suppliers who buy carbide inserts and steel bodies from different sources, Shareate **manufactures its own carbide and assembles the tools in-house**. This vertical integration ensures the tungsten carbide grade is perfectly matched to the tool's application, resulting in superior wear resistance and predictable tool life.
* **Global "Local" Support**: One of the biggest pain points for international buyers is after-sales service for Chinese products. Shareate has addressed this by establishing **local service companies and warehouses** in the USA, Australia, Canada, and Chile (Drillco). You get Chinese cost-efficiency combined with local technical expertise and faster delivery.
* **R&D Driven Durability**: In mining, every hour of downtime is a massive loss. Shareate invests nearly **5%** of its annual revenue into **R&D** (holding **470+ patents**). Their tools are designed using advanced forging and heat-treatment technologies to ensure they don't just "fit" the machine, but actually last longer in tough formations like iron ore or copper mines.
* **Certified for High-Risk Industries**: Holding **API Q1** and **ISO 9001** certifications, Shareate operates at the highest level of quality management required by the **oil and mining industries**. Their status as a **publicly-traded company** ensures a level of transparency and financial stability that smaller workshops cannot provide.
