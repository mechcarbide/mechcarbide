## Company Profile

### Legacy & Expansion

Established in 1981 by John Goodrum, Basic Carbide has evolved from a modest startup into a global manufacturing force with customers in **over 50 countries**. What began as a family-run operation has expanded into a multi-facility corporate network, strategically acquiring and retrofitting sites in Pennsylvania to house distinct production divisions. Today, the company is successfully led by **the second and third generations** of the Goodrum family, a rare feat in the industry that guarantees **long-term business stability and consistent management values**.

### Strategic Manufacturing Footprint

Basic Carbide operates four specialized facilities across Pennsylvania, totaling **over 117,000 square feet** of manufacturing space:

* **Lowber, PA (HQ & Nickel Division):** The heart of their corrosion-resistant carbide production.
* **Buena Vista, PA (Cobalt Division):** Dedicated to large-scale cobalt binder preforms.
* **Sutersville, PA (Press Plant):** High-volume automatic pressing center for cost-effective mass production.
* **Latrobe, PA (Finishing Plant):** A massive engineering hub for grinding and final machining.

This strategic division of labor allows Basic Carbide to optimize efficiency for each product type, offering **quick turnarounds** for both "off-the-shelf" standard rings and custom-engineered components.

## Capabilities

### Production

**Specialized Material Production**

* **Nickel Binder Expertise:** Operating the industry's only known facility dedicated solely to Nickel carbides, capable of producing seal rings up to 20 inches in diameter.
* **Cobalt & Large Preforms:** The Buena Vista facility specializes in Cobalt binder grades, handling some of the largest preforms available in the global market.

**Advanced Finishing & Machining**

Basic Carbide's **in-house finishing capabilities** are among the most extensive in the US, eliminating the need for third-party machine shops. Their arsenal includes:

* **Precision Grinding:** A vast array of CNC OD/ID grinders, Surface grinders (Blanchard up to 36"), and Thread grinders.
* **Complex Shaping:** Equipped with Sodick Wire & Ram EDMs for intricate geometries that traditional grinding cannot achieve.
* **Super-Finishing:** Lapping and Honing capabilities for mirror-finish requirements (critical for seal rings).

**Quality Assurance (ISO 9000 Certified)**

* **Traceability:** Full tracking from lot-specific raw materials to final operators.
* **Inspection:** Lab equipped with CMMs (Brown & Sharp), Optical Comparators, and Contour Measuring Systems, ensuring every shipped part meets N.I.S.T. traceable standards.
