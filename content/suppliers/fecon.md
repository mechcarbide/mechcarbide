Founded in 1992 in Ohio, Fecon is a leading manufacturer of **land management and natural resource recovery equipment.** With more than 30 years of experience, the company is known for producing some of the most durable **mulching heads** in the industry, along with a broad range of specialized equipment designed for forestry, land clearing, and environmental applications. Fecon emphasizes **technology, innovation, and operational efficiency** to deliver high-value, long-lasting products. With **over 25,000 mulcher heads** supplied worldwide, Fecon continues to support professionals through reliable equipment and strong customer partnerships.

## Product Categories

### 1. Attachments

* **Bull Hog® Mulching Heads:** Fecon's signature line of forestry mulching attachments, available for skid steers, excavators, and tractors.
* **Forestry Attachments:** Includes deck mowers, grapples, and tree shears, each designed and customized for full compatibility with different drive systems and horsepower ranges. These attachments deliver strong ROI and are supported by Fecon's dealer and factory network.
* **Stump Grinders:** The Stump Hog series offers grinding solutions for skid steers, excavators, and tractors of various sizes.

### 2. Mulching Tractors

Fecon manufactures three dedicated mulching tractors: **FTX150-2, FTX200, and FTX300.**

### 3. Parts & Accessories

Fecon supplies a complete range of wear parts, replacement components, and accessories to support all attachments and mulching tractors.

## Fecon Fusion Technology

* Fusion is Fecon's **advanced intelligent control system** designed to optimize mulching performance in real time. 
* It automatically tunes the attachment to the carrier based on dynamic factors such as temperature, altitude, and hydraulic efficiency, eliminating the need for manual adjustments.
* Fusion maximizes power consistency and minimizes recovery time for peak productivity.
* Through the **Fusion App**, operators can monitor live performance dashboards, switch operating modes, and receive software updates to keep equipment running at its best.
