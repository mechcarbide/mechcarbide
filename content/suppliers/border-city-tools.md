Border City Tools is a long-established U.S. manufacturer supplying cutting components for stump grinders, landscaping equipment, and mining applications. Founded in 1954, the company provides **stump grinder teeth, pockets, bolts**, and related parts made with **high-grade carbide** for consistent cutting performance. These replacement parts are compatible with major equipment brands such as **Vermeer, Rayco, and Carlton**. Its product range also includes **mining bits** and **construction drilling bits**. 

Known for several patented designs, Border City Tools focuses on durable, cost-effective solutions and aims to support operators with reliable replacement parts suited for demanding field conditions.

## Product Categories

![Border City Tools product range](/uploads/border-city-tools-product-range.jpg "Border City Tools Product Range")

* Stump grinder teeth compatible with commonly used stump grinder brands such as Carlton, Vermeer, Rayco, and Promark.
* Carbide-tipped construction bits designed for foundation drilling.
* Carbide-tipped mining drill bits used in coal and auger mining operations.
