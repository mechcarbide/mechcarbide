## Editor's Review

While many suppliers just sell raw rods, Extramet sells **efficiency**. Their core philosophy revolves around 'Blank Preparation', taking raw carbide and grinding it into precision **Step Blanks** or **Formed Blanks** before it even reaches your factory.

### Why this matters to you:

* **Cut Your Cycle Time:** By purchasing pre-ground step blanks from Extramet, tool makers can skip the time-consuming rough grinding process and go straight to CNC finishing. This significantly reduces your machine cycle time and scrap rates.
* **Swiss DNA, American Muscle:** Founded in Switzerland in 1980 and expanded to a massive facility in Latrobe, PA, Extramet combines Swiss dimensional precision with American high-volume production capacity.
* **Aerospace Focus:** They are one of the few suppliers who truly understand the carbide substrate requirements for machining modern aerospace composites (CFRP/Titanium). If you make aerospace tools, their specialized grades are a must-have.

## Capabilities

### Advanced Blank Preparation

Extramet's "Sweet Spot" is delivering material that is ready for your final finishing.

* **Step Blanks:** They supply ground step blanks that match your final tool geometry. This helps tool makers **reduce lead times** and minimize sludge/scrap in their own shops.
* **Cut-to-Length:** Precise cutting services to provide exact rod lengths needed.

### Centerless Grinding Excellence

As a specialist in centerless grinding, Extramet achieves:

* **Tolerances:** As tight as **0.0002 inches**, ensuring perfect concentricity for high-speed rotation tools.
* **Finishes:** Options ranging from standard ground to highly polished mirror finishes (critical for medical and food applications).

### CNC Cylindrical Grinding

For more complex geometries, their CNC Cylindrical department offers:

* **Complex Profiles:** Ability to grind intricate forms and tapers on carbide rods.
* **Automation:** New automated loading systems installed in 2021 ensure high-volume consistency and faster turnaround.

### Industry-Specific Solutions

* **Aerospace:** Specialized substrates for machining CFRP/Titanium stacks.
* **Medical:** Micro-bonding capabilities to graft carbide tips onto steel/titanium shafts for surgical instruments.
* **Food Safe:** High corrosion-resistant grades suitable for harsh cleaning solvents in food processing.
