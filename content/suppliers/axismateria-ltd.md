## üìåWhy Choose AXISMATERIA?

**Best For:** Manufacturers of micro-precision tools (PCB drills, electronics) and high-end tool makers demanding absolute Japanese material consistency.

* **Sumitomo Group Vertical Integration:** As a subsidiary of Sumitomo Electric Hardmetal, AXISMATERIA benefits from **a fully integrated supply chain**. The group manages the entire process from raw tungsten refining to carbonizing and sintering. For the buyer, this guarantees **a stable supply of raw materials**, even during global shortages, and ensures that the chemical composition of the carbide remains identical from batch to batch, year after year.
* **World-Leading Micro-Drill Technology:** Holding a top global market share in **blanks for PCB drills**, AXISMATERIA specializes in "super small diameter" rods. Their manufacturing process focuses on the thorough elimination of foreign substances and internal defects. This level of purity is critical for micro-tools, where even microscopic inclusions can cause immediate breakage. This expertise translates into superior runout control and transverse rupture strength for all their small-diameter products.
* **Advanced R&D via PMIC:** Customers gain access to cutting-edge metallurgy through the company's close cooperation with the **Powder Metallurgy Innovation Center (PMIC)** of Sumitomo Electric. This allows AXISMATERIA to rapidly deploy the latest grade technologies, developed by one of the world's most advanced material science labs, into standard commercial rods and custom die/mold blanks, giving tool makers a competitive edge in performance.
