## 📌 Why Choose Shandong AID?

**Best For**: Mining and tunneling contractors who are tired of frequent downtime due to bit wear and are looking for high-end picks.

* **Patented Hardfacing Technologies (2-5x Service Life)**: The biggest pain point in mining is the frequent replacement of bits. AID's **self-developed hardfacing technology** creates **a dual-layer carbide ring** around the pick body. This unique "**tough exterior, steel core**" structure ensures the pick lasts significantly longer than standard models, directly reducing your downtime and energy costs.
* **Proven "Import-Substitute" Quality**: In a **coal mining pick bidding project** of Huainan Mining Group, AID outperformed over ten domestic and international competitors to **rank #1**. Their picks are engineered using **42CrMo alloy steel** bodies and premium **cemented carbide** (WC-Co) tips, ensuring the bit doesn't snap under high-impact conditions.
* **Specialized in Hard Rock & Complex Geology**: Their new **carbide grits and carbide inserts hardfacing** technologies are specifically designed for the toughest environments. These bits feature high-hardness wear particles embedded in the tip to prevent premature chipping when hitting hard rock or corrosive layers.
* **Certified Reliability for Large Operations**: AID is a qualified supplier for major energy giants like **China Coal** and **Shaanxi Coal**. All products carry necessary safety certifications (MA/Safety Marks). With an **in-house R&D center** and **32 patents**, they provide a level of technical security that small-scale workshops simply cannot match.
