## 📌 Why Choose Cadence?

**Best For**: Medical device OEMs and high-precision film/foil converters requiring scientifically verified sharpness and extreme tool longevity.

* **Submicron Carbide Edge Engineering**: Cadence utilizes high-performance **submicron grain tungsten carbide** to overcome the brittleness common in traditional carbide tools. Their specialized grade selection (varying Cobalt content from **6% to 15%**) allows them to sharpen edges to a razor-like finish that can outlast standard steel by **up to 80 times**. This provides a critical balance of wear resistance and shock toughness, significantly reducing machine downtime in high-volume slitting applications.
* **Proprietary 9-Axis CNC Sharpening**: Cadence builds its own **9-axis precision sharpening machines** from scratch. This unique technology, backed by multiple patents, enables the creation of complex blade geometries and multiple-step grinding sequences. For the buyer, this results in unmatched edge consistency and the ability to produce custom blade shapes that standard manufacturing processes cannot achieve.
* **IncisionLab™ Analytical Verification**: Through their dedicated **IncisionLab™**, Cadence moves beyond subjective sharpness to scientific measurement. They use SEM imaging (up to **24,000x magnification**) and penetration force evaluation to quantify cutting performance. This data-driven approach allows customers to optimize incision force and compare material properties with mathematical precision, ensuring the final product meets exact clinical or industrial requirements.
* **Advanced Design for Manufacturing (DFM)**: With over **75 engineers** on staff, Cadence excels at simplifying complex designs to improve quality and reduce costs. They specialize in converting multi-part welded assemblies into high-performance, one-piece solutions using proprietary honing technologies. Their rapid prototyping service can turn a design into a functional tool in days rather than months, accelerating the development cycle for critical surgical and industrial components.
