## 📌 Why Choose Telos?

**Best For**: Furniture manufacturers and industrial distributors looking for high-performance finger joint cutters and planer blades that can handle extremely hard woods (Rosewood, Ebony, etc.) without chipping.

* **Elite Material Selection (Virgin Powder)**: For their high-end finger joint and planer series, Telos uses **100% virgin fine-grain carbide from Luxembourg**. This ensures the tool maintains a sharp edge even when cutting dense hardwoods, preventing the edge-cracking that ruins expensive timber.
* **Advanced Precision Hardware**: Telos has equipped its production line with high-precision grinders from **Taiwan and German-made perspective inspection systems**. This allows them to control the straightness of their planer blades within **<0.05mm**, ensuring a mirror-smooth wood surface that often eliminates the need for secondary sanding.
* **Hardwood Processing Specialist**: If you are processing **Mahogany, Sandalwood, or Ebony**, Telos is a rare specialist. Their TCT planer blades use a unique **seamless technology** and **internal stress relief** process to ensure the blade body remains stable and crack-free under the high heat and pressure of hardwood cutting.
* **Export-Friendly Logistics & Paperwork**: Telos is very experienced in **international trade**. They don't just make tools; they actively help buyers reduce total costs by applying for **Form E or CO (Certificate of Origin)** and finding the most economical shipping routes. Their sales team is skilled in technical consulting, making the communication process smooth and professional.
