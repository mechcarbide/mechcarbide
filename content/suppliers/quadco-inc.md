## 📌Why Choose Quadco?

**Best For**: Professional logging contractors and forestry fleet managers who require high-speed production and low per-hour operating costs in abrasive environments.

* **Four-Sided Rotatable Efficiency**: The patented **Quad Tooth®** System revolutionized the industry by allowing operators to **rotate a single tooth four times** before replacement. This maximizes the utility of each carbide edge, ensuring the machine maintains a razor-sharp cut for longer periods and significantly reducing the total expenditure on wear parts over time.
* **Super Tip® Abrasive Resistance**: For operations in sandy or gritty soil, the Super Tip® technology utilizes **extra-thick carbide** with a strategic **insert overlap**. This design specifically prevents "sand blow-by", a common issue where abrasive particles erode the steel shank. As a result, it extends the structural life of the tooth and protects the saw disc from premature wear.
* **Spiral Bite Limiter Precision**: The latest M-series mulchers incorporate a **spiral-shaped bite limiter** on the drum. By regulating the depth of each cut, this technology prevents the drum from "stalling" on heavy vegetation and ensures the material is spread evenly. This results in a much more consistent, sawdust-like finish and protects the carrier's hydraulic system from extreme shock loads.
* **Universal Carrier Adaptability**: Quadco attachments, such as the **M62**, feature a **built-in universal boom adapter** with **swappable pins and sleeves**. This solves the compatibility headache for contractors and rental fleets, allowing a single high-performance mulcher to be quickly moved between different 22-35 ton carriers without permanent modifications or lengthy downtime.
