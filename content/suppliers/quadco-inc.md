Quadco is a Canadian manufacturer specializing in **forestry cutting systems and attachment heads**, established in 1989 and headquartered in St-Eustache, near Montreal. The company operates additional facilities in British Columbia and Washington State, with its U.S. division based in Lexington, South Carolina. 

Known for innovation and holding more than **40 patents**, Quadco is recognized globally for the **Quad Tooth System®** and other cutting tools, saw discs, and forestry attachments. Its products are available through OEMs and distributors across North America and international markets. Quadco focuses on high-quality engineering, continuous improvement, and long-term customer partnerships within the forestry industry.

## Product Categories

![Quadco product range](/uploads/quadco-product-range.jpg "Quadco Product Range")

### 1. Attachments

* **Mulching heads:** 45QDM, 56QDM, M32, M42, M52, M62.
* **Felling Heads:** 18C, 20SC, 22B 22C, etc.
* **Harvesting heads:** 10000XT, 12000XT, 2000T, 3000T, etc.
* **Scarification heads:** M24.A, M36.B, M46.B, T26.B, T28.A, T35.B.
* **Seeders:** S35.A.
* **Planters:** P11.B, P12.B, P22.B.

### 2. Chain Sharpeners

Quadco manufactures chain sharpeners designed for **grinding chainsaw chains**, including the **Combimatic, Grindomatic V12, Sensomatic, and Triplematic** models.

### 3. Teeth

* **Mower and mulcher teeth**, made from steel or carbide, are compatible with well-known brands such as Denis Cimaf, Fecon, FAE, and Rayco.
* **Grinder teeth**, produced from alloy steel with carbide overlay, are suitable for equipment from Vermeer, Morbark, Diamond Z, CBI, and others.
