## Quick Facts

**Company Name**: Zhejiang Baotong Construction Machinery Co., Ltd. (浙江宝通工程机械有限公司)

**Location**: Wenzhou, Zhejiang Province (Strategic location: 30km from Wenzhou Airport and Port)

**Established**: 2013

**Business Type**: Manufacturer / Factory

**MOQ Policy**: Flexible. They welcome custom processing based on samples or drawings, making them suitable for both small-batch specialized orders and volume supply.

**Export Experience**: Yes. Their primary markets are Europe and the USA.

**Specialty**: Integrating R&D and production of finished engineering wear tools, rather than just raw carbide tips.

## 📦 Products & Customization Capabilities

**Standard Products**: Road milling bits, grader bits, coal mining bits, roadheader picks, trencher teeth, bullet teeth, and various tool holders/bases.

**Forestry & Recycling**: Specialist in mulcher teeth, tub & horizontal grinder tips, and stump grinder teeth.

**Customization (OEM/ODM)**: Highly capable of manufacturing based on customer-provided drawings or physical samples.

**Surface Treatment**: Hard-facing (surface reinforcement) is available for forestry and construction bits to extend service life in high-abrasion environments.

## 📌 Why Choose Baotong?

**Best For**: International distributors or equipment operators seeking cost-effective finished wear tools for road construction, mining, and forestry mulching.

* **Ready-to-Use Finished Tools**: Many Chinese suppliers only produce the carbide blanks, leaving buyers to find another factory for steel bodies and brazing. Baotong provides the entire assembly—carbide tip, steel body, and tool holder. This **"one-stop" finished product approach** saves you significant sourcing time and assembly costs.
* **Proven in High-End Markets**: With a focus on the **European and American** markets, they understand that a bit failing on-site costs more than just the part itself. Their production follows **ISO9001 standards**, ensuring that their bits can handle the rigorous demands of road milling and heavy-duty mulching.
* **Flexibility for "Niche" Parts**: In the world of construction and forestry, there are countless variations of teeth and bits. Baotong’s willingness to work from **samples or drawings** makes them an ideal partner for sourcing hard-to-find replacement parts for older machinery or specialized trenching equipment.
* **Logistics Efficiency**: Located in the **Wenzhou manufacturing hub**, they are only 30km from major transport links. This proximity to the port translates into faster dispatch and more competitive shipping rates for international buyers.
