## 📌 Why Choose Robit?

**Best For**: Large-scale mining and underground construction projects operating in challenging rock formations that require extreme penetration rates and high-tier reliability.

* **Automated Precision & Button Retention Technology**: To eliminate the common industry problem of button failure and premature wear, Robit has invested heavily in **state-of-the-art robotized production cells**. Their sophisticated automated **button insertion machinery** ensures consistent tension and stability for every tungsten carbide button. For the operator, this translates to a significantly longer tool life and predictable performance, even when used with the industry's most powerful hydraulic drifters.
* **Scandinavian Steel & Granite-Tested Durability**: Robit products are engineered using **high-alloy Scandinavian steel** and **compact, homogenous tungsten carbide**. Developed and continuously tested in the unforgiving granite of Scandinavia, these tools are built to handle the hardest rock conditions on earth. This "**designed for the extreme**" approach means customers worldwide benefit from a higher penetration rate and a lower cost-per-meter drilled.
* **Sustainable & Material-Efficient Manufacturing**: Addressing the growing demand for green procurement, Robit's tools are manufactured from **100% recyclable materials**, prioritizing steel produced through environmentally friendly processes that reduce CO2 emissions by up to 80%. This commitment to sustainability doesn't compromise quality; instead, it optimizes the microstructure of the tools, providing a high-performance product with a significantly reduced environmental footprint.
* **Global Logistics with Triple-Continent Manufacturing**: With strategically located manufacturing units in **Finland**, **South Korea**, and the **UK**, plus service points in **seven countries**, Robit ensures short lead times and precise deliveries. This multi-continent manufacturing capability allows them to react quickly to changing market demands. In addition, it ensures that high-quality drilling consumables are available locally on every continent, reducing inventory pressure for their distributors.
