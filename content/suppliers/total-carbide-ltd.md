## Company Profile

### A Legacy of British Engineering

Total Carbide is the modern evolution of two historic British pioneers: **Hoybide** (est. 1950s) and **Teco** (est. 1930s). Merged in 2000 and rebranded in 2009, the company is now a key part of **Versarien plc**, an advanced materials group listed on the AIM market.

### State-of-the-Art Facility

Operating from a custom-built facility at Westcott Venture Park (relocated in 2016), Total Carbide combines **70 years** of heritage with cutting-edge technology. As the UK's leading independent manufacturer, they serve a global client base with a focus on **Oil & Gas**, **Aerospace**, and Industrial Wear applications. Their affiliation with Versarien plc places them at the forefront of material innovation, constantly exploring new frontiers in tungsten carbide performance.

* **Complex Geometries:** This unique process enables the production of carbide components with intricate internal forms (e.g., choke valves) that are impossible to machine using conventional methods.

* **Large Format Pressing:** Capable of producing rings and components up to **420mm** in diameter, catering to large-scale industrial **pump and seal** requirements.
* **HIP Sintering:** All products undergo Hot Isostatic Pressing to eliminate porosity, ensuring maximum density and strength.
* **Precision Finishing:** A comprehensive finishing department offering CNC cylindrical grinding, EDM wire/die-sinking, and lapping to 2 light-bands flatness.
