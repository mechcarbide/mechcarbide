## üìå Why Choose CMT Orange Tools?

**Best For:** Professional cabinet makers, furniture manufacturers, and distributors requiring industrial-grade consistency with rapid global availability.

* **Trademark Orange PTFE Coating:** CMT is instantly recognizable by its orange color, but this is more than just branding. This specialized **Non-stick PTFE coating** is engineered to withstand the intense physical stresses of woodworking. It effectively prevents resin and residue build-up on the tool body and reduces friction heat. For the woodworker, this means the blade runs cooler, cuts cleaner without burning the workpiece.
* **Fully Automated "Made in Italy" Precision:** To solve the common industry problem of batch inconsistency, CMT has transitioned its Udine facility to a **completely automated production process**. From turning steel shanks to brazing specially formulated micrograin carbide tips, every step is managed by advanced CNC robotics. This ensures that whether you buy a router bit today or next year, the balance, concentricity, and sharpened edge quality remain identical.
* **Material Science:** Quality cuts start with the substrate. CMT refuses to cut corners on raw materials, utilizing only **solid bar stock steel** and premium **micrograin carbide**. This combination provides the structural integrity needed to handle high-RPM industrial applications while maintaining a razor-sharp edge longer than standard carbide. This results in "perfectly sharpened edges" that leave a smooth finish on solid woods, laminates, and plywood.
* **24-Hour Logistics Efficiency:** For distributors and high-volume users, stock availability is critical. CMT maintains over **7,000 standard tools** in inventory, managed by **20+ automated vertical storage systems** in factories worldwide. This advanced logistics infrastructure allows for rapid order processing, with most tools ready for prompt shipment **within 24 hours**. This minimizes your inventory holding costs while ensuring you have the right tool exactly when production demands it.
