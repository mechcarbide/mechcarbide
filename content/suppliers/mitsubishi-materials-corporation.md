## üìå Why Choose Mitsubishi Materials?

**Best For:** Automotive & Aerospace Manufacturers processing heat-resistant superalloys, and Die & Mold Shops requiring extreme precision for hardened steels.

* **Advanced Coating Science:** Machining ultra-hard steels often leads to rapid tool oxidation and wear. Mitsubishi's **IMPACT MIRACLE series** utilizes a proprietary **(Al, Cr, Si)N coating** that offers superior oxidation resistance and lubricity. For **mold makers**, this technology enables high-speed, high-precision machining of hardened materials with significantly lower vibration and noise, resulting in smoother surface finishes and longer tool life.
* **Automated Consistency:** At their flagship Tsukuba Plant, Mitsubishi invested over **$200 million** to create a 24/7 **automated** carbide production facility. This robotic pressing and transport system guarantees absolute batch-to-batch consistency. Buyers of indexable inserts receive identical performance from the first insert to the millionth.
* **Cost-Efficient Drilling:** Traditional solid carbide drills are expensive to replace. The **DXAS Exchangeable Head Drills** combine the centering accuracy of a solid drill with the cost-efficiency of a replaceable tip. This allows factories to renew the cutting edge without discarding the shank, significantly reducing tool inventory costs while maintaining rigid, high-precision hole making.
* **Your Personal Craftsman Studio:** Mitsubishi positions itself not just as a vendor, but as a "**Craftsman Studio**." Through their global network of **9 MTEC (Technical Centers)**, they offer **on-site Production Line Diagnoses**. Their technical staff will physically visit your factory, analyze your entire machining process, and propose specific improvements using CAD/CAM simulations. This transforms them from a tool seller into a process optimization partner committed to your success.
