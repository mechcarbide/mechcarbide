## 📌 Why Choose Pacific Tool?

**Best For**: Professional tool distributors and industrial woodworking shops seeking premium replacement blades that offer OEM-level precision and significantly longer service life.

* **Exceptional Surface Finish (One-Pass Planing)**: For woodworkers, the biggest pain point is the need for secondary sanding. Pacific Tool's blades achieve a straightness of less than **0.02mm** (on 82mm blades). The result is a **smooth, mirror-like finish on softwoods** that often eliminates the need for polishing.
* **Premium Material & Extended Lifespan**: They use high-grade **HSS imported from Japan** and premium **virgin tungsten carbide** for their TCT range. Combined with their unique **brazing and heat treatment processes**, their blades offer a **30% to 50%** longer service life compared to standard market alternatives, ensuring fewer blade changes and higher productivity.
* **Military-Grade Precision & Automation**: Unlike workshops relying on manual grinding, Pacific Tool uses self-developed **6-axis integrated grinding systems**. This ensures that every single blade has a perfectly uniform edge roughness and superior sharpness, maintaining benchmark quality across high-volume batches.
* **30 Years of Proven Global Reliability**: Supplying global leaders like **Bosch** and **Makita** for decades isn't just about price; it's about passing the most rigorous quality audits in the power tool industry. When you source from Pacific Tool, you are accessing the same supply chain quality as the world's top brands.
