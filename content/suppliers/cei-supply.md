Founded in 1978, CEI Supply provides stump grinder teeth, pockets, wheels, bolts, upgrade kits, brush chipper knives, and related cutting accessories to tree care and landscaping professionals. The company carries well-known industry brands such as **Greenteeth, Leonardi, and DK2, along with its own CEI product line**. 

With a focus on reliability and operational efficiency, CEI Supply offers a broad product selection, competitive pricing, and fast fulfillment to help reduce equipment downtime. The company emphasizes customer support and aims to deliver consistent quality for users seeking dependable stump grinder parts.

## Product Categories

### 1. Stump Grinder Parts

![CEI stump grinder teeth](/uploads/cei-supply-product.jpg "CEI Stump Grinder Teeth")

CEI Supply provides an extensive selection of professional **stump grinder parts, including teeth, bolts, pockets, wheels, and performance upgrade kits**. The product range features leading brands such as [Greenteeth](https://www.mechcarbide.com/suppliers/green-manufacturing-inc/), Leonardi, DK2, and the company's own CEI teeth series.

### 2. Brush Chipper Parts

The company offers brush chipper knives suitable for various applications, available from trusted brands such as DK2 and Simonds International.

### 3. Tree Cutting Accessories

CEI Supply also carries a variety of tree cutting and maintenance accessories, including Chain Locker chainsaw gear, Virginia Abrasives grinding discs, Blue Gator ground protection mats, Drainzit™ oil-changing tools, lubricants, safety barricades, and tachometers.

## Shipping

CEI Supply provides free standard ground shipping on orders **over $199**, with certain exclusions. Unless otherwise specified, orders are shipped using the most economical method. Standard ground orders placed **before 2 p.m. (CST) Monday–Friday** ship the same business day; orders placed after that time ship the next business day. Delivery typically takes **1–5 business days**, depending on location and carrier conditions.

Overnight and 2-day shipping are available for orders placed by **2 p.m. (CST) Monday–Thursday**, with weight limits up to **10 lbs**. For international shipments, including Canada, additional fees or brokerage charges may apply. Customers should report any damages or shortages immediately and retain all packaging for inspection. For assistance or shipment inquiries, CEI Supply's support team is available to help.

## Return Policy

Customers must obtain prior authorization before sending any returns, as a CEI Supply–issued **Sales Return Order (SRO) number** is required on the outside of the package. Items must be returned within **60 days** of delivery and be in **new, unopened, and unused** condition unless defective or damaged during shipping. All returns are subject to a **20% restocking fee** and must be shipped by mail. Once returned products are received, CEI Supply may take up to **7 business days** to process the request and issue the appropriate credit.
