## 📌 Why Choose Ridge Carbide Tool?

**Best For**: Custom cabinet makers, professional woodworkers, and architectural millwork shops seeking glue-ready finish cuts and fast-turnaround custom tooling.

**Ultra-Tight Tolerances**: Ridge Carbide engineers their saw blades with a low side taper of just **.001 on the carbide teeth** and maintaining a remarkably true saw body (**runout of .000 to .0015 inches**). This exactness produces super smooth, jointed edges right off the saw, allowing you to glue boards together immediately and saving countless hours of secondary sanding and jointing.

**1200-Grit Professional Sharpening & Repair Service**: Finding a reliable local sharpening service that doesn't ruin expensive blades is a common pain point for craftsmen. Operating as a full-service repair center, Ridge Carbide's "Tool Doctors" use a rigorous **1200-grit grinding process** to restore dull, warped, or wobbly tools. They accept and expertly sharpen cutting tools across all brands, breathing new life into tired equipment and maximizing your long-term tooling investments.

**Rapid Factory-Direct Custom Tooling**: When specialized restoration jobs or custom architectural projects demand unique profiles, long lead times for custom bits can stall production. Operating a fully equipped USA factory, Ridge Carbide offers **100% American-made custom router bits**, **shaper cutters**, and **profile knives** with exceptional speed. Their standard delivery is a swift **3-4 weeks**, with an **Express Service** option of just **5-7 days**, keeping your urgent projects on schedule.
