**Established**: 2006

**Business Type**: Manufacturer

**Production Capacity**: 10+ million pieces annually with automated production lines.

**MOQ Policy**: Flexible. They offer cost-effective cutting solutions and support non-standard customization, making them suitable for both bulk buyers and specialized custom needs.

**Export Experience**: Yes. Their products are exported to over 60 countries across Europe, America, and Southeast Asia.

## 📦 Products & Customization Capabilities

**Standard Products**:

* **Woodworking**: Reversible inserts (square, rectangle, etc.), edge-banding machine blades, straight planer blades (TCT/HSS), spiral cutter heads, profiling inserts, saw tips, and router bits.
* **Digital Cutting**: Replacement blades for ZUND, ESKO, IECHO, ATOM, and other mainstream cutting machines.
* **Industrial Slitting**: Circular blades (for lithium batteries, corrugated cardboard, plastic, and rubber) and industrial razor blades (3-hole, slotted).
* **Others**: Carbide rods, scrapers, and rotary burs.

**Customization (OEM/ODM)**: Highly responsive to non-standard cutting tool designs. They specialize in tailoring tool geometry and material grades to specific industrial cutting requirements.
